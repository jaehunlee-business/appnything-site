Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

Blossum AI is a product of Appnything. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect information when you use the app and related support services.

1. Information we collect

Depending on how you use Blossum AI, we may collect the following:

Account information. When you create an account, we collect your email address and name.

Photos. Blossum AI requires access to photos or your camera to perform facial analysis. Photos you submit are uploaded and stored securely in our cloud storage in order to provide analysis results and maintain your history within the app.

AI analysis data. When you submit a photo, it is processed by Google Gemini, a third-party AI service, to generate ratings and feedback related to skin quality, facial symmetry, proportions, and features. The results of this analysis are stored in your account.

Daily routine and goal inputs. We collect content you enter into the app, including your daily routines, habits, and other inputs you choose to add.

Optional profile information. You may optionally provide information such as your gender, age, and personal goals to help personalize your experience.

Usage information. We may collect information about how you use the app, including feature interactions, session activity, diagnostics, and crash reports, to improve performance and reliability.

Purchase and subscription information. If you purchase a subscription, the Apple App Store or Google Play may provide us with transaction information such as subscription status, entitlement status, and product identifiers. We do not collect or store your payment card information directly.

Examples of data that may be collected include:

• Email address and name

• Photos submitted for facial analysis

• AI-generated analysis results

• Daily routine and habit entries

• Optional profile details (gender, age, goals)

• User ID or account identifier

• Subscription and purchase status

• Crash and diagnostics data

2. How we use information

We use collected information to:

• provide and improve the app and its features;

• perform AI-powered facial analysis on submitted photos;

• store and display your analysis history and routine data;

• authenticate users and secure accounts;

• process subscriptions and restore purchases;

• respond to support requests;

• monitor performance and fix issues;

• prevent fraud, abuse, or misuse; and

• comply with legal obligations.

We do not use your personal data or photos for third-party advertising, nor do we use them to track you across apps or services owned by other companies.

Important notice about AI analysis. Analysis results generated by Blossum AI are produced by an automated AI system and are for personal, informational purposes only. They do not constitute medical, dermatological, or professional advice. You should not rely on them as a substitute for professional consultation.

3. How information may be shared

We do not sell your personal information or photos.

AI processing. Photos you submit are sent to Google Gemini for analysis. Google may process this data in accordance with their own privacy policies and terms. We encourage you to review Google's privacy policy before using the analysis feature.

Service providers. We may share data with trusted providers such as Firebase and RevenueCat to operate, maintain, and improve the app.

Platform providers. Apple and Google may process account, payment, and subscription-related information according to their own policies.

Legal requirements. We may disclose data if required by law, regulation, legal process, or government request.

Protection and security. We may share information when necessary to protect users, investigate misuse, enforce our terms, or protect the app and our services.

Business transfers. Information may be transferred in connection with a merger, acquisition, financing, or sale of assets.

4. Photos and sensitive data

Because Blossum AI processes facial photos, we take extra care with this data. Photos and analysis results are stored securely and are associated only with your account. We do not share your photos with third parties beyond the AI processing described above.

You may request deletion of your photos and associated analysis data at any time by contacting us at blossum.ai@gmail.com.

5. Data retention

We retain data only as long as necessary to provide services, maintain your account, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce agreements. You may request deletion of your data at any time.

6. Your rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or request a copy of your data, including photos and analysis results.

To make a request, contact blossum.ai@gmail.com.

We process data based on legitimate interests, contract performance, and user consent where applicable.

7. Account deletion

You may request deletion of your account and all associated data — including photos and analysis history — by contacting blossum.ai@gmail.com.

Where available, you may also delete your account directly within the app. Upon deletion, your personal data will be removed within a reasonable timeframe, unless retention is required by law or for legitimate security or fraud-prevention purposes.

8. Data security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational safeguards to protect your information, including your photos. However, no method of transmission or storage is completely secure.

9. Children's privacy

Blossum AI is not intended for children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information — including photos — from children under 13.

10. International data transfers

Your data, including photos submitted for analysis, may be processed and stored in countries other than your own, including where our service providers and AI partners operate.

11. Third-party services

Blossum AI uses the following third-party services, each operating under their own privacy policies:

• Firebase — authentication, cloud storage, and crash diagnostics

• RevenueCat — subscription and purchase management

• Google Gemini — AI-powered facial analysis

We encourage you to review the privacy policies of these providers before using related features.

12. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will update the "Last updated" date when changes are made. Continued use of the app after changes become effective means you accept the updated policy.

13. Contact

For questions, support requests, or privacy-related requests, contact blossum.ai@gmail.com.

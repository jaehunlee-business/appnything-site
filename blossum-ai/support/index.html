Support

Blossum AI is a product of Appnything. We're here for bug reports, account questions, feature feedback, and privacy-related requests.

Contact

Email support

Contact us at blossum.ai@gmail.com.

To help us resolve your issue faster, include your device type, operating system version, app version, and a short description of what happened.

Typical response time

We aim to respond within 2 business days.

What we can help with

Login issues, analysis questions, subscription help, bugs, and general app feedback.

Common support topics

Analysis issues

If the facial analysis is not loading or returning unexpected results, include the photo format and steps to reproduce.

Account and access

If you are having trouble signing in or accessing your data, include the email address associated with your account.

Subscriptions and purchases

For billing questions, include the platform used for purchase and any relevant receipt or order details.

Feature requests

We welcome ideas that can improve Blossum AI. Feel free to share suggestions via email.

Privacy and data requests

If you have questions about privacy, or would like to request deletion of your data including photos and analysis history, contact us at blossum.ai@gmail.com with the subject line Privacy Request.

Last updated: April 16, 2026

Checklist App is a product of Appnything. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect information when you use the app, website, and related support services.

Depending on how you use Checklist App, we may collect the following:

Information you provide directly. This includes your email address, account details, checklist content, habits, goals, preferences, reminders, and any information you choose to submit.

Usage information. We may collect information such as feature interactions, session activity, diagnostics, crash reports, and device-related data to improve performance, reliability, and security.

Purchase and subscription information. If you purchase features, the Apple App Store or Google Play may provide us with transaction information such as subscription status, entitlement status, and product identifiers. We do not collect or store your credit card or payment information directly.

Information from third-party services. We use services such as Firebase (authentication, analytics, cloud storage, diagnostics), RevenueCat (subscription management), and platform providers like Apple and Google for account and payment-related services.

Examples of data that may be collected include:

• Email address

• User-generated content such as habits, goals, and checklist entries

• User ID or account identifier

• Purchase and subscription status

• Crash and diagnostics data

2. How we use information

We use collected information to:

• provide and improve the app;

• store and sync your data;

• authenticate users and secure accounts;

• process subscriptions and restore purchases;

• respond to support requests;

• monitor performance and fix issues;

• prevent fraud, abuse, or misuse; and

• comply with legal obligations.

We do not use your personal data for third-party advertising or for tracking you across apps, websites, or services owned by other companies.

3. How information may be shared

We do not sell your personal information.

Platform providers. Apple and Google may process account, payment, and subscription-related information according to their own policies.

Legal requirements. We may disclose data if required by law, regulation, legal process, or government request.

Protection and security. We may share information when necessary to protect users, investigate misuse, enforce our terms, or protect the app and our services.

4. Data retention

We retain data only as long as necessary to provide services, maintain your account, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce agreements.

5. Your rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or request a copy of your data.

To make a request, contact appnything.checklist@gmail.com.

We process data based on legitimate interests, contract performance, and user consent where applicable.

You may request deletion of your account and associated data by contacting appnything.checklist@gmail.com.

Where available, you may also delete your account directly within the app. Upon deletion request, your personal data will be removed within a reasonable timeframe, unless retention is required by law or for legitimate security or fraud-prevention purposes.

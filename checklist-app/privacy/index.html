Checklist App Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 25, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Checklist App (“the App”), operated by Appnything (“we,” “us,” or “our”), handles information when you use the App.

1. Information We Collect

Checklist App may collect information you provide directly, such as habit entries, check-in activity, preferences, and support messages you send to us.

The App may also collect limited technical information necessary to operate and improve the service, such as device type, operating system version, app version, and basic analytics or crash data if enabled.

2. How We Use Information

We use information to:

  • Provide and maintain the App
  • Save your settings and app experience
  • Improve performance, reliability, and usability
  • Respond to support requests and user feedback

3. Data Storage

User data may be stored locally on your device and, where applicable, through third-party services used to support authentication, syncing, analytics, crash reporting, subscriptions, or app infrastructure.

4. Third-Party Services

The App may rely on third-party tools or service providers to support features such as analytics, authentication, cloud storage, crash reporting, subscriptions, or notifications. Those services may process information according to their own privacy policies.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share limited information with service providers only as needed to operate, maintain, and improve the App, or if required by law.

6. Your Choices

You can stop using the App at any time. Depending on how the App is configured, you may also be able to delete data from within the App or by removing the App from your device.

7. Children’s Privacy

Checklist App is not intended for children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

8. Security

We take reasonable measures to protect information, but no method of transmission or storage is completely secure.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any updates will be posted on this page with a revised “Last updated” date.

10. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at support@appnything.com.

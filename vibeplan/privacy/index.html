Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

VibePlan is a product of Appnything. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect information when you use the app and related support services.

1. Information we collect

Depending on how you use VibePlan, we may collect the following:

Account information. When you create an account, we collect your email address and name to identify and authenticate your account.

Location information. VibePlan uses your device's location while the app is open to generate relevant trip and event plans based on your current area. We do not collect location data in the background when the app is not in use.

Trip and event planning inputs. We collect information you provide when building a plan, including the number of people, available time, number of stops, preferred transportation, budget, food preferences, and any other details you choose to enter. Completed plans are saved to your account for future reference.

Usage information. We may collect information about how you interact with the app, including feature usage, session activity, diagnostics, and crash reports, to improve performance and reliability.

Purchase and subscription information. If you purchase a subscription, the Apple App Store or Google Play may provide us with transaction information such as subscription status, entitlement status, and product identifiers. We do not collect or store your payment card information directly.

Examples of data that may be collected include:

• Email address and name

• Device location (while app is in use)

• Trip and event plan details and preferences

• Saved plans and history

• User ID or account identifier

• Subscription and purchase status

• Crash and diagnostics data

2. How we use information

We use collected information to:

• provide and improve the app and its planning features;

• generate personalized trip and event recommendations based on your location and inputs;

• save and sync your plans across devices;

• authenticate users and secure accounts;

• process subscriptions and restore purchases;

• respond to support requests;

• monitor performance and fix issues;

• prevent fraud, abuse, or misuse; and

• comply with legal obligations.

We do not use your personal data or location for third-party advertising, nor do we share it to track you across apps or services owned by other companies.

3. How information may be shared

We do not sell your personal information.

Service providers. We may share data with trusted providers such as Supabase, Firebase, and RevenueCat to operate, maintain, and improve the app.

Maps and location services. VibePlan uses a maps and location API to display maps and generate location-based recommendations. Location data passed to this service is used solely for generating your plan and is subject to the provider's own privacy policy.

Platform providers. Apple and Google may process account, payment, and subscription-related information according to their own policies.

Legal requirements. We may disclose data if required by law, regulation, legal process, or government request.

Protection and security. We may share information when necessary to protect users, investigate misuse, enforce our terms, or protect the app and our services.

Business transfers. Information may be transferred in connection with a merger, acquisition, financing, or sale of assets.

4. Location data

VibePlan accesses your device location only while you are actively using the app. We do not track or collect your location in the background. Location data is used to generate plans relevant to your current area and is not shared with third parties beyond the maps and location service provider described above.

You may revoke location permissions at any time through your device settings. Doing so may limit certain planning features within the app.

5. Data retention

We retain data only as long as necessary to provide services, maintain your account, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce agreements. Saved plans and account data are retained until you request deletion or delete your account.

6. Your rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or request a copy of your data, including saved plans and account information.

To make a request, contact vibeplan.app@gmail.com.

We process data based on legitimate interests, contract performance, and user consent where applicable.

7. Account deletion

You may request deletion of your account and all associated data — including saved plans and preferences — by contacting vibeplan.app@gmail.com.

Where available, you may also delete your account directly within the app. Upon deletion, your personal data will be removed within a reasonable timeframe, unless retention is required by law or for legitimate security or fraud-prevention purposes.

8. Data security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational safeguards to protect your information. However, no method of transmission or storage is completely secure.

9. Children's privacy

VibePlan is not intended for children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

10. International data transfers

Your data may be processed and stored in countries other than your own, including where our service providers operate.

11. Third-party services

VibePlan uses the following third-party services, each operating under their own privacy policies:

• Firebase — authentication and crash diagnostics

• Supabase — cloud storage and database

• RevenueCat — subscription and purchase management

• Maps / Location API — map display and location-based recommendations

We encourage you to review the privacy policies of these providers before using related features.

12. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will update the "Last updated" date when changes are made. Continued use of the app after changes become effective means you accept the updated policy.

13. Contact

For questions, support requests, or privacy-related requests, contact vibeplan.app@gmail.com.

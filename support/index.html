Contact

Email support

Contact us at appnything.checklist@gmail.com.

To help us resolve your issue faster, include your device type, operating system version, app version, and a short description of what happened.

Typical response time

We aim to respond within 2 business days.

What we can help with

Login issues, sync issues, subscription questions, bugs, and general app feedback.

Common support topics

App issues

If the app is crashing, not saving correctly, or behaving unexpectedly, include steps to reproduce the issue.

Account and access

If you are having trouble signing in or accessing your data, include the email address associated with your account.

Subscriptions and purchases

For billing questions, include the platform used for purchase and any relevant receipt or order details.

Feature requests

We welcome ideas that can improve Checklist App. Feel free to share suggestions via email.

Privacy and data requests

If you have questions about privacy or would like to request deletion of your data, contact us at appnything.checklist@gmail.com with the subject line Privacy Request.

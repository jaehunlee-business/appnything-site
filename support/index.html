Support

Need help?

For support, bug reports, account questions, or general inquiries related to Appnything apps, contact us at support@appnything.com.

Contact

Email: support@appnything.com

Response time: We aim to respond within 2 business days.

What to include

  • The app name
  • Your device type and OS version
  • A short description of the issue
  • Screenshots if relevant

FAQ

How do I request help?

Email support@appnything.com with the app name and a short explanation.

How do I report a bug?

Please include steps to reproduce the issue, screenshots if possible, and your device details.

Where can I find privacy information?

Each app has its own privacy policy page linked from the homepage.
